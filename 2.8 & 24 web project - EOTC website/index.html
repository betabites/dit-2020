EOTC Week

Tawa College E.O.T.C Week

As part of the Year 10 programme students will be participating in four days of Education Outside the Classroom (EOTC). The focus of this programme is one of citizenship and social awareness. The four days of activities in which your son/daughter will participate are as follows: Community Service, Ferg's Kayaks Course, Laser Paintball and H2O Xtream, Mount KauKau Walk.
